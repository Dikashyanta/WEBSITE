Despite Nepal's strides towards modernity and legal reforms, caste discrimination remains a pervasive and deeply entrenched issue. Rooted in centuries-old Hindu traditions, the caste system has long divided Nepalese society into hierarchical groups, perpetuating inequality and marginalization. This blog explores the historical context, current realities, and ongoing efforts to combat caste-based discrimination in Nepal.
